DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL

Document Retrieval



  • Document Retrieval:
    • Search for and retrieve any document from public records

  • Mortgage Search:
    • Find a specific mortgage and retrieve copies.

  • Deed of Trust Search:
    • Find a specific deed of trust (another name for a mortgage) and retrieve copies.

  • Deed Search:
    • Find a specific deed and retrieve copies.

  • Loan Report:
    • Copies of all open mortgages and related documents.

  • Assignment Search:
    • An “assignment” is when one bank sells a mortgage or loan to another bank. Find all assignments that pertain to a specific mortgage.

  • Release Search:
    • A “release” is a document saying that the mortgage/loan has been paid in full. Find a specific release and obtain a copy.

  • Satisfaction Search:
    • A “satisfaction” is a document saying that the mortgage/loan has been paid in full. Find a specific satisfaction (usually called a “release” on the document) and obtain a copy.

  • Discharge Search:
    • A “discharge” is a document saying that the mortgage/loan has been paid in full. Find a specific discharge (usually called a “release” on the document) and obtain a copy.

  • Lien Search:
    • Locate any open liens against one specific person.

  • Environmental Lien Search:
    • An environmental lien is a legal document filed on a piece of property when it has been discovered that the land has been exposed to toxic or harmful elements. A search of the public records to find any environmental liens that may have been filed on the property.

  • Court Case Document Retrieval:
    • Locate a specific court case and retrieve copies of all documents in the file.

  • Judgment Search:
    • Locate any open judgments against one specific person.

  • UCC Search:
    • Uniform Commercial Code (UCC) filings allow creditors to notify other creditors about a debtor’s assets used as collateral for a secured transaction. UCC liens filed with Secretary of State offices act as a public notice by the “creditor” of the creditor’s interest in the property. Locate any open UCCs against a specific person or entity.

  • HOA Search:
    • A homeowner’s association (HOA) is an organization in a subdivision, planned community, or condominium building that makes and enforces rules for the properties and its residents. A search of public records to locate the name of the HOA or Condominium Owners Association (COA) of a property.

  • Easements, CCRs, Rights of Way:
    • An exhaustive search of the public land records taken to locate any EASEMENTS, COVENANTS, CONDITIONS, RESTRICTIONS, RIGHTS OF WAY. We will search back within the time frame that you require.

  • Plat Copy:
    • A plat is a plot of land. A plat map is a diagram and is used to show how your purchased property is divided within your county, city, or neighborhood. Retrieve a particular plat map for a property.

  • Federal or State Tax Lien Search:
    • Search for any federal/state tax liens for one particular name.

  • Property Tax Search:
    • Quick report of who owns a property along with the deed and legal description.

  • Document Retrieval:
    • Locate and provide the property tax information for one particular property. How much is owed, is it paid, etc.

  • Power of Attorney Search:
    • Locate and retrieve a POA.
